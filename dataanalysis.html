Data analysis:

  1. (Note this capability is restricted to administrators only.)
  2. Click on the ‘Data Analysis’ button on the search screen.
  3. Select the desired attributes for the data you wish to analyze.
  1. Optionally select a date, period of time, and table attribute to compare to. Users have the option to select periods of either one week, one month, or one year. Weeks are Sunday to Sunday, with the range beginning on the last sunday to have occurred before the selected date (note selecting a Sunday indicates the first day of the selected week). Months are taken from the first day of the month of the date selected to the last day of that month, and years are taken as the first day of the year of the date selected, to the last day of that year.
  2. Optionally select a test type from the drop down bar.
  3. Optionally select a patient from the drop down bar.
  1. Select the ‘Submit’ button to execute the data analysis.
  1. Should you have selected a date, you will be presented with the option to scale the selected period of time either up or down, still based around the originally selected date.
  2. Select your chosen scaling option by clicking on the button, and the data will be regathered.
  1. Click the ‘Back’ button when you are finished.
